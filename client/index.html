More than just a conference

The International Conference on Carbon Neutral Technology for Sustainable Development and the 2023 Taiwan-India Exchange Workshop on Sustainable Renewable Energy and Smart Energy Storage Technology will be held from September 4 to 6, 2023, at the IIT Delhi (India). This conference and workshop aim to bring together experts, researchers, industry professionals, and policymakers from around the world to explore and discuss innovative solutions for achieving carbon neutrality and sustainable development.

The purpose of the International Conference on Carbon Neutral Technology for Sustainable Development is to build a platform on renewable production and utilization, smart energy storage, and CCUS based technologies, circular economy and others sustainable technology in industrial and academic area. This Symposium is in line with Taiwan's science and technology development policy, six major axes and 5+2 industrial innovation goals. This workshop would create a win-win situation with more exchanges and cooperation opportunities between Taiwan and India.
