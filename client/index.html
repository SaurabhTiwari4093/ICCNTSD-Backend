More than just a conference

The International Conference on Carbon Neutral Technology for Sustainable Development and the 2023 Taiwan-India Exchange Workshop on Sustainable Renewable Energy and Smart Energy Storage Technology will be held from September 4 to 6, 2023, at the IIT Delhi Seminar Hall. This conference and workshop aim to bring together experts, researchers, industry professionals, and policymakers from around the world to explore and discuss innovative solutions for achieving carbon neutrality and sustainable development.

The event will focus on carbon-neutral technologies, which play a vital role in mitigating climate change and reducing greenhouse gas emissions. Participants will engage in in-depth discussions and presentations on various topics such as renewable energy, smart energy storage, energy efficiency, clean transportation, and sustainable development practices.
