Platinum Sponsor

The Platinum sponsor will have the following opportunities and privileges:

  • Free registration for two participants, including all benefits
  • One invited speech of a sponsor representative or presentation - 10 min
  • A full page avert next to the title page of the conference proceedings
  • Display space in the conference hall

Golden Sponsor

The Golden sponsor will have the following opportunities and privileges:
